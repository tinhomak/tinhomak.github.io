Question

Read the question at Irish math olympiad year 2020 Q5

Given a, b, c > 0, Prove that

$$(\frac{a}{b+c} + \frac{b}{c+a}) ^ \frac{1}{7} + (\frac{b}{c+a} + \frac{c}{a+b}) ^ \frac{1}{7} + (\frac{c}{a+b} + \frac{a}{b+c}) ^ \frac{1}{7} \geq 3$$

Answer by AM-GM inequality

Divide both side by 3 and applying AM-GM inequality, it is sufficient to prove that:

$$((\frac{a}{b+c} + \frac{b}{c+a}) ^ \frac{1}{7} (\frac{b}{c+a} + \frac{c}{a+b}) ^ \frac{1}{7} (\frac{c}{a+b} + \frac{a}{b+c}) ^ \frac{1}{7}) ^ \frac{1}{3} \geq 1$$ $$(\frac{a}{b+c} + \frac{b}{c+a}) (\frac{b}{c+a} + \frac{c}{a+b}) (\frac{c}{a+b} + \frac{a}{b+c}) \geq (1 ^ 3) ^ 7 = 1$$

Tricky part \( \frac{a}{b+c} + \frac{b}{c+a} \)

Let u, v, w be \( a+b \), \( b+c \) and \( c+a \) respectively. They are all positive.

That results in \( a=(u-v+w)/2 \), \( b=(v-w+u)/2 \), \( c=(w-u+v)/2 \).

Formula (1) holds due to AM >= GM

$$\frac{1}{2} ( \frac{v}{w} + \frac{w}{v}) \geq \sqrt{ (\frac{v}{w} \frac{w}{v}) } = 1 $$

Formula (2) holds also due to AM >= GM

$$\frac{1}{2} (\frac{u}{w} + \frac{u}{v}) \geq \frac{u}{\sqrt{vw}}$$

Adding Formula (1) and (2) results is:

$$\frac{1}{2} (\frac{v+u}{w}) + \frac{1}{2} (\frac{w+u}{v}) \geq 1 + \frac{u}{\sqrt{vw}}$$

Substract left hand side by 1/2 twice, and substrce right hand side by 1.

$$\frac{1}{2} (\frac{v+u}{w} -1) + \frac{1}{2} (\frac{w+u}{v} -1) = \frac{v+u-w}{2w} + \frac{w+u-v}{2v} \geq \frac{u}{\sqrt{vw}}$$ $$\frac{b}{w} + \frac{a}{v} \geq \frac{u}{\sqrt{vw}} = \frac{a+b}{\sqrt{(b+c)(c+a)}}$$

Similar cases to handle tricky part of \( (b/w) + (c/u) \) and \( (c/u) + (a/v) \)

Back to the question

Return to the inequality to be proven:

$$(\frac{a}{b+c} + \frac{b}{c+a}) (\frac{b}{c+a} + \frac{c}{a+b}) (\frac{c}{a+b} + \frac{a}{b+c}) \geq 1$$

It is sufficient to prove that:

$$ (\frac{a+b}{\sqrt{(b+c)(c+a)}}) (\frac{b+c}{\sqrt{(c+a)(a+b)}}) (\frac{c+a}{\sqrt{(b+c)(a+b)}}) \geq 1 $$

But it is equal now. The denominator is:

$$ \sqrt{(b+c)(c+a)} \sqrt{(c+a)(a+b)} \sqrt{(b+c)(a+b)} = (a+b)(b+c)(c+a) $$

Summary

The whole proof is:

$$ \frac{ (\frac{a}{b+c} + \frac{b}{c+a}) ^ \frac{1}{7} + (\frac{b}{c+a} + \frac{c}{a+b}) ^ \frac{1}{7} + (\frac{c}{a+b} + \frac{a}{b+c}) ^ \frac{1}{7} }{3} $$ $$ \geq ((\frac{a}{b+c} + \frac{b}{c+a}) (\frac{b}{c+a} + \frac{c}{a+b}) (\frac{c}{a+b} + \frac{a}{b+c})) ^ \frac{1}{21} $$ $$ \geq (\frac{a+b}{\sqrt{(b+c)(c+a)}} \frac{b+c}{\sqrt{(c+a)(a+b)}} \frac{c+a}{\sqrt{(b+c)(a+b)}}) ^ \frac{1}{21} $$ $$ = 1 ^ \frac{1}{21} $$

The number 7 in the question can be replaced by any positive number, as 1 to the power of positive number is 1.

Equality holds if and only if \(a=b=c\)
